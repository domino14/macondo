speedtest - Check if your computer is fast enough to volunteer

Usage:
    speedtest

This command runs a brief simulation to measure your computer's performance
in nodes per second (nps). A minimum of 7500 nps is recommended for
volunteering.

If your computer is too slow, try:
    - Closing other applications
    - Ensuring your computer is plugged in (not on battery)
    - Using a more powerful computer
